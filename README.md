# HR Dashboard

 Overview

This HR Dashboard provides a complete view of employee-related insights such as headcount, salary, leave balance, gender distribution, age distribution, job roles, and employee joining trends.

The dashboard is designed to help HR teams and management make better workforce decisions by tracking important employee metrics in one place.

---

 Dashboard Features

 KPI Cards

The dashboard contains the following KPI cards:

* Total Headcount: 161 employees
* Average Leave Balance: 16.42 days
* Average Salary: 54.23K
* Employees with Leave Balance Over 20 Days: 29

 Visualizations

 1. Headcount by Job Title

* Displays the number of employees in each job role.
* Helps identify which departments or positions have the highest workforce.

 2. Gender Distribution

* Shows the percentage of male and female employees.
* Female Employees: 54.66%
* Male Employees: 45.34%

 3. Job Title Filter

* Allows users to filter the dashboard based on specific job roles.
* Makes it easier to analyze employee details by department or position.

 4. Age Distribution of Staff

* Displays employee count across different age groups.
* Segmented by gender for better workforce analysis.

 5. Employee Joining Trend

* Shows the cumulative number of employees added over time.
* Useful for understanding company growth and hiring patterns.

 Key Insights

* The organization has a total workforce of 161 employees.
* Female employees make up a slightly larger portion of the workforce.
* Packaging Associate and Production Operator are among the highest headcount roles.
* The average salary across employees is 54.23K.
* Most employees fall between the age group of 28 to 35 years.
* Hiring has steadily increased over the years.


 Purpose of the Dashboard

The main purpose of this dashboard is to:

* Monitor workforce distribution
* Analyze employee demographics
* Track leave and salary patterns
* Support HR decision-making
* Understand hiring trends over time
